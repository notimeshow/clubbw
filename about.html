Our Story

There is this general saying “We all live in a black and white world” and yes keeping the same in mind your city is getting a new destination “Club BW”, the first of it’s kind in the capital. The energies in the universe too consist of the BW concept and for those high on energy looking for a place to let your hair down and have a time of your life here opens the gate to the most luxurious nightclub of Delhi “Club BW”. This place gives you the perfect taste of lounge and nightclub under one roof. As you enjoy your sip here you are transported to a world which is the clubs mantra “-“Forget about the worries, and let the party begin!” This is the place for endless entertainment.

Are You Ready To Rock?
We Planned Mind-Blowing Entertainment For You

The club opens for you a whole new world of some delectable platters; a bar with some of the best and world’s finest spirits on to flaunt and a lounge that lets you take your place in the heavens, night after night. The wine boasts of some thoughtfully put together names along with an excellent range of whiskey selection and food cooked the modern bistro style. While you guzzle cocktails created by our exceptional mixologists, the bone shaking sound system and production is bound to drag you to the floor. Music elevates a level higher with live stage performances as you indulge in some food, drink, music and unmitigated mirth! Spread over an area of 4000 sq. ft the best from the profession will be there to serve you. True to it’s name a classic vintage black and white scheme and Fuchsia drapes make the place a funkier affair. Interesting and impressive architectural design along with extravagant décor makes the club one of its kind in the city. This bar is sure to catch everyone’s attention as its designed according to international standards. This club is an Ode to the long awaited and much needed nightclub revival in the Capital. One stop for the perfect nightlife: Mind blowing sound, magnificent lights, and distinguished ambience.

Drapes that harmonize cultural milieus along with distinctive concept, “Club BW” is big enough to fill its gaping girth. So as you enter lofty gates you are welcomed to a world of finesse, luxury., leisure and fun time ahead. Unwind your senses and head towards The Suryaa for Luxury Awaited clothed in the form of Club BW!
